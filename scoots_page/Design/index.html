Main Page

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to draw buisness to our rentals. We want to focus on rentals that are ahead of the dates the scooters are needed. This will provide time for us to prepare and also give us an estimate of the differing needs for differnt seasons. We want the site to be useable for tourists, professional bookers (cruise companies), and locals alike. Acurate and extensive information is critical so that our customers know what they are getting before they book.

Target Audience

Our target audience for this site will be fun loving tourists and cruise attendants. We want them to be well informed about our provided options and how those options will help them to reach thier vacation goals. Our target audience is active and seeks adventure and so the site should refect that. We also want to reach the kind of tourist who wants to see things that other tourists don't see. It might be a good idea to mention our local staff and their ability to help with finding the "lesser known" areas.
