Take a Tour

Scuba Diving

Go scuba diving off the Coast of Cozumel. We'll take you out on our boat and show you the spots where the good stuff happens. We offer full and half day packages. Certification required. Call for pricing.

Snorkeling

Want to hang out underwater but don't have a scuba certification? Try snorkeling. We'll still take you out on our boat but we'll stay a little closer to shore. We offer full day, half day, and 2 hour packages. Call for pricing.

Beach Tours

Would you rather stay on the land? Book a beach tour with us and we'll take you to several of Cozumel's lesser known beaches. We offer packages that include three, two, and one location. Call for pricing.
