Rental Agreement Info

So you want to make a reservation!?

Please fill out the form below and one of our representatives will call you within one hour. During the call we will answer any questions you may have and we will go over all the legal information. You will be required to pay a $50 deposit in order to hold your vehicle so please have your payment information ready. (For more information about payment please see our 'Rentals' page.) Also, there will be paperwork that we will need to be e-signed before we can hold your vehicle. Please provide an email address or have another form of contact avaiable for electronic paperwork. Lastly, proof of legal ability to drive will be necessary so please have your Drivers License ready as well.We look forward to working with you and thank you for using ~Scoots.
