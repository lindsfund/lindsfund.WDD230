Target Audience

Personas

Local Mother with Kids

This woman is between the ages of 19-26 and lives in the area. She has at least one child under the age of five. She is most likely a stay at home mom but we don't want to exclude the working moms. She will be relativey tech savy be able to navigate the web and apps on her phone. Her phone is where she will most likely be visiting from most of the time. She will be looking for activities for her children that will get them the time they need outside with other children. She will also be looking for other moms to socialize with at the same time. She will be looking for weather appropriate activities because of the ages of her children.

Single active individuals

These will be individuals between the ages of 16-40 who are either new to the area or visiting. They will be looking for outdoor activities to enguage in either in small groups or by themselves that are weather appropriate. They will most likely be accessing the site by laptop or tablet in order to plan a vacation or virtually explore the area.

Senarios

  • Is there a playgroup meeting today before it rains? What ages are the kids?
  • It's snowing so I can't go fishing. What else is there to do when it's snowing?
  • The roads are too slick for my bicycle because of the rain. Where is a good place to run instead?
  • I'm moving to the area and want to know what people do in the winter when it's cold.
  • I'm new in town and want a group to go walking with but only when it's warmer than 50 degrees.
