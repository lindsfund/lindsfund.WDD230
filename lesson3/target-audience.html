Target Audience

Personas

Emmalee

Emmalee is a 28 year old, stay at home mother of 4 children. The oldest is a 7 year old boy, next a 5 year old girl and last twin boys age 3. Emmalee home schools her children and is always on the look out for outdoor PE activities. She also wants to find activities where her children can interact with other kids their ages. She also would not be dissapointed to find other women that are in a similar situation to her. Her boys are gifted at finding mud and bathing in it so she would like to know ahead of time if she should bring extra towels. A precipitation forcast for the area is a must. She is always on the go so she will most likely be viewing the page from her phone.

Cullen

Cullen is a single 32 year old male. He is new to the area and was drawn here by the vast array of available outdoor activities. He likes to be prepared and so he plans his activities ahead of time. He will most likely be viewing the site from his laptop but will also need to be able to access it from his phone while he is out and about. There is not much that will keep Cullen indoors and so weather reports are a must. He also is affraid of lightning so he will want ot know if there have been any sightings in the area.

Senarios

  • Is there a playgroup meeting today before it rains? What ages are the kids?
  • It's snowing so I can't go fishing. What else is there to do when it's snowing?
  • The roads are too slick for my bicycle because of the rain. Where is a good place to run instead?
  • I'm moving to the area and want to know what people do in the winter when it's cold.
  • I'm new in town and want a group to go walking with but only when it's warmer than 50 degrees.
