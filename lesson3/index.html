Main Page

Purpose

The main purpose of this site is to provide viewers with the weather information necessary to plan their weekly activities. They will be able to see the community's weekly schedule and the weather, Sunshine or snowflakes, and plan the week accordingly. This will require multiple pages for community plans and links as well as a calander of the forcast. Links to sync with the users personal calander might be helpful as well. If there are no community events planned for the week, it might be possible to have a place to initiate a sort of small group meeting. (i.e-a playdate at the park, small running group at the track, yoga at the school gym.)
